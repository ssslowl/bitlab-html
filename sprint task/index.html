Khabib to Konor: it's the same thing

When Khabib Nurmagomedov speaks, everybody listens because he is arguably one of the best UFC fighters in history. The whole scandal between Jorge Masvidal and Colby Covington reached a boiling point that pushed Jorge to attack Colby outside of the octagon. For some reason, Covington thinks he can behave like a WWE heel in the UFC whilst forgetting the octagon is a place where fighters actually go out to take your head off. This isn't pretend like other combat sports, this is people truly risking their lives for a paycheck. Khabib Nurmagomedov already had a similar situation with Conor McGregor and things also got heated up between the two fighters.
